Company Profile
A National Leader in Electrical Construction

MYR Group Inc. (NASDAQ: MYRG) is one of the largest electrical construction firms in North America, providing a full spectrum of electrical construction services to clients across the commercial, industrial, and utility sectors. Through our subsidiary CSI Electrical Contractors, we deliver comprehensive renewable energy construction solutions including utility-scale solar, wind collector systems, battery energy storage, and high-voltage substation and interconnection work. Headquartered in the Western United States, CSI Electrical has established itself as a premier contractor for some of the most complex and high-profile renewable energy projects in the nation, with a particular emphasis on projects throughout the Pacific Northwest, Southwest, and Mountain West regions.

Core Capabilities
Full-Spectrum Renewable Energy Electrical Construction
☀

Solar Farm Construction

Utility-scale photovoltaic installation including tracker systems, fixed-tilt arrays, DC wiring, combiner boxes, inverter pads, and AC collection systems for projects ranging from 50 MW to 500+ MW.

🌀

Wind Farm Collector Systems

Underground and overhead medium-voltage collector systems, turbine pad electrical infrastructure, step-up transformers, and meteorological tower wiring for wind energy facilities up to 300+ MW.

⚡

Battery Energy Storage (BESS)

Complete electrical balance-of-plant for lithium-ion and emerging battery technologies, including DC power systems, inverter installation, HVAC electrical, fire suppression wiring, and grid interconnection.

⚖

Substation Construction

Greenfield and brownfield substation construction from 34.5 kV to 500 kV including foundations, steel erection, bus work, equipment setting, grounding grids, control buildings, and relay protection systems.

🔌

High-Voltage Interconnection

Transmission-level interconnection services including gen-tie lines, switching stations, and point-of-interconnection infrastructure to integrate renewable generation assets to the bulk power grid.

💡

Medium-Voltage Distribution

Design-build and design-assist medium-voltage electrical distribution for renewable energy facilities, including pad-mounted switchgear, underground duct banks, and overhead distribution lines up to 34.5 kV.

🖥

SCADA & Controls

Supervisory control and data acquisition system installation, fiber optic communication networks, RTU programming, DAS integration, meteorological monitoring, and real-time plant performance systems.

✅

Commissioning & Testing

Comprehensive commissioning services including relay testing, insulation resistance testing, hi-pot testing, transformer oil analysis, power quality analysis, and coordinated system energization procedures.
